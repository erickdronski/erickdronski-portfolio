About Me

We never really know where life will take us. Moments and situations define us, and the choices we make along with the experiences we collect shape the next chapter. What we think is the “right” path often isn’t the one we end up walking.

I’m grateful to say my journey has been anything but ordinary. Every twist and turn, both personally and professionally, has taught me to zoom out and ask: What will I take from this moment? How will it translate into the next thing I’m building? How does it fit into the trends, patterns, and constant changes in the world around me?

I started in banking, picturing myself as the Patagonia vest guy networking on the golf course. But life had other plans. I shifted into SaaS as an Account Executive, then into IT as a Strategic Consultant, all while exploring social media — partly for personal reasons, partly because my wife happens to be a social media and photography savant.

Somehow, all these experiences blended perfectly. They shaped the skills I’ve built, the work I’ve done, and most importantly the people I’ve been able to connect with around the world. Along the way, my presence grew: 17K+ connections on LinkedIn, 55K+ followers on TikTok, and a steadily growing audience on X. But more valuable than numbers has been the conversations, stories, and ideas shared with others.

I’m not a content wizard. I don’t have some secret formula. I just put myself out there. And now, I’m still building, still learning, and this site is just another step in that journey.

If you’ve made it this far, I hope you explore, learn more about me, and maybe even connect. After all, the best chapters are the ones we write together.
